Commands

/connect

Connect your Discord channel to Revolt and Telegram simultaneously. You need to provide the IDs for both platforms to create the bridge.

/connect <revolt_channel_id> <telegram_channel_id>
/disconnect

Disconnect the current Discord channel from all connected platforms. Execute this command in the channel you want to disconnect.

/disconnect

Privacy Policy

Sensitive Information We Have Access To

To redirect Pepbridge conversation messages from the Discord threads back to Pepbridge, the application has access to read the content of messages inside the server channels where the application has access. We don't store message content data on our servers or any third-party service.

Information We Collect

We store your account data necessary for the service to function: Channel ID, Server ID, Webhook ID, Webhook Token.

Additionally, we temporarily cache message IDs to enable cross-platform reply functionality. This allows messages and replies to be properly mapped between different platforms, ensuring conversations remain threaded and coherent across Discord, Telegram, and Revolt. These cached message IDs are automatically pruned after 3 weeks, as they're only needed for reply functionality.

How We Use Your Data

Service Provision: we use collected data to provide and improve the Bot's features and services.

Data Sharing

We do not cache, log or otherwise store your message content. Only message IDs are temporarily cached for cross-platform reply functionality. After our software forwards information between platforms, the message content is permanently deleted from our systems.

Data Security

We do not share any of your personal information to any third party or use it in any way other than to provide you this service.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of any material changes.
